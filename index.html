Frequently Asked Any Questions

Feel free to ask details?

How often should I groom my pet?

Depends on breed and coat; long hair needs more frequent grooming.

What are the benefits of professional pet grooming?

Healthier coat/skin, comfort, better appearance, early problem detection.

Can I groom my pet at home?

Yes, basic grooming like brushing and nail trims can be done at home.

How do I measure my pet for a shirt?

Chest girth, back length, neck girth.

What size shirt should I get for my pet?

Check the brand's size chart, sizes vary.

What tools do I need for home grooming?

Brushes, nail clippers, pet shampoo, conditioner (optional).
